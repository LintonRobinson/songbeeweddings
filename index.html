Who We are

Our Melody, Your Story

At Songbee, we collaborate with wedding videographers to create custom music perfectly aligned with each couple’s unique love story. Our compositions enhance the emotional depth of your wedding films, forging a seamless blend between visuals and sound—so you can deliver an unforgettable viewing experience.

By offering personalized, story-inspired soundtracks, you stand out in a crowded market, and enhance the value of your packages. With Songbee’s custom compositions at the heart of your films, you’ll strengthen your brand, elevate your artistry, and boost your revenue by delivering unforgettable, emotionally resonant wedding memories.

  • Elevate the narrative of your videos with unique, personalized compositions.
  • Offer clients a distinctive feature that sets your work apart from competitors.
  • Charge higher rates by including custom music as a value-added service.

Seamless Integration With Songbee

1. Offer Custom Music to Your Clients

Introduce your clients to Songbee’s custom music service as part of your wedding videography package. They’ll love the idea of a personalized song crafted just for their love story.

2. Collaborate to Choose Preferences

Using personal details and their love story, your clients can select their song preferences; including style, genre, mood, and even the vocalist’s gender. This song is designed to capture the couple’s essence and the emotions of their special day.

3. Deliver a Complete Musical Experience

Receive a one-of-a-kind custom song crafted from your clients’ preferences, along with a professionally adapted version integrated into their wedding video’s soundtrack—chosen by you. Enhance your work with music that makes their memories truly unforgettable.

FAQ's

How does the custom music process work?
Your clients share their story and music preferences. We craft a unique song based on their input, which is also adapted to seamlessly fit their wedding video.
Why should I offer custom music to my clients?
Custom music adds a personal, emotional touch to wedding videos, helping you stand out, impress clients, and charge premium rates.
Will I have control over how the music fits into my video edits?
Absolutely. You decide how and where the music is used to ensure it complements your creative vision and workflow.
Can the music be used beyond the wedding video?
Yes, the custom song can also be used for special moments like processions, first dances, and other meaningful events.
